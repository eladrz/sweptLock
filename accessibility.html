Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction
SweptLock is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website, ensuring equal access for people with disabilities.

Compliance Status

Our website endeavors to conform to level Double-A (AA) of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, in accordance with the Israeli Standard (IS 5568) for web accessibility.

Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard Navigation: The website is designed to be fully navigable using only a keyboard.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Our site structure is built to be compatible with standard screen-reading software.
  • Visual Readability: We maintain standard color contrast ratios and avoid flashing or rapidly blinking elements.

Physical Office Accessibility

SweptLock operates its platform entirely as a digital service. We do not host a public reception area. All support and inquiries are conducted online.

Contact Our Accessibility Coordinator

If you experience any difficulty in accessing any part of this website, or if you need assistance, please contact our Accessibility Coordinator, Elad Rehani. We aim to respond to all accessibility inquiries within 48 hours.
